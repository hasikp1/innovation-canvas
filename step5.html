Market Research Prompt

I'm a student working on an innovation project and need to validate my idea before proceeding. Please help me research existing solutions and identify gaps in the market.

MY INNOVATION IDEA:

Product/App Name: [Solution name will be loaded]

Core Problem: [Problem will be loaded from Step 1]

Target Market: [Target users will be loaded from Step 2]

Proposed Solution: [Selected solution will be loaded from Step 4]

Key Features: [Key features from refinement will be loaded]

RESEARCH REQUESTS:

1. Existing Solutions Analysis: Find 5-10 similar products/apps, compare features, pricing, strengths/weaknesses

2. Market Gap Analysis: What problems do existing solutions NOT solve?

3. Innovation Opportunity: Is my idea truly unique? What would make it significantly better?

4. Competitive Positioning: How can I differentiate from existing solutions?

VALIDATION QUESTIONS:

  • Does this solve a real, significant problem?
  • Is there sufficient market demand?
  • Can I realistically compete with existing solutions?

This research will help me either refine my innovation or pivot to a more viable opportunity.

Physical Products

Transform sketches into realistic product images

Step-by-Step Instructions
1

First, complete market research above to validate your idea

2

Prepare your hand-drawn sketch or detailed description of your physical product

3

Take a clear photo of your sketch (if applicable) or write a detailed description

4

Open ChatGPT and use the enhanced prompt template below

5

Upload your sketch image or paste your description

6

Review and refine the generated image as needed

Best Practices
  • Always start with competitive research first
  • Make your sketches clear with labeled parts
  • Include business context (market size, revenue model)
  • Specify target audience and use case clearly
  • Mention colors, textures, and finishing details
  • Focus on what makes your product unique

Apps & Digital Tools

Open Claude and use the enhanced prompt template below

5

6

Request specific screens or the entire user journey

Best Practices
  • Always start with competitive research first
  • Define your app's primary purpose clearly
  • Include business model and revenue strategy
  • List key features and user actions
  • Specify target device (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Focus on unique value proposition
